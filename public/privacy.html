Privacy
Policy.

01. Data Collection

SENCI does not collect personal identity information. We only monitor anonymized hardware performance metrics to optimize the system flux.

02. Security

System optimization data is encrypted at the hardware layer using military-grade AES-256 protocols. No data is stored on remote servers.

03. Local Storage

Optimization profiles are stored locally on your device and are cleared upon app termination or manual override.

Protocol.

01. Dynamic Throttling

SENCI automatically manages power distribution. It prevents hardware overheating by dynamically throttling performance before critical heat thresholds are reached.

02. Battery Health

Our optimization engine is designed to extend battery lifecycle. It reduces idle voltage spikes that contribute to long-term lithium degradation.

03. Hardware Guard

SENCI operates at a safe layer of the OS, ensuring that no permanent damage can happen to the device chips or memory units.
